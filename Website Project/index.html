Welcome to Doggo Pals!

Welcome to Doggo Pals! A safe haven for your fur babies to escape and have fun!

We aim to offer the best service for the best prices. Head over to our services section to find out more.

Here at Doggo Pals, we pride ourselves on being the most active day care centre.

We take your dog on a supervised by you appointment here at our doggo HQ. Once we find the right routine for your doggo, we can suggest our different services packages for you to choose from.

We also know it is important to try before you buy! Therefore, if you are unsure we offer a FREE day care day, so you can decide without a prior commitment.

Our Awards

Doggo Pals are proud to be top prize winners in the Pet Dog Day Care category by the Pet and Animal Federation 2021 for the fifth year running!

When awarded with the prize, our CEO and founder, Tanya Holden, said: "Doggo Pals are proud to win this prestigious award. The team have worked endlessly to provide excellent services to our canine friends and canine owners. This would not be possible without my team, so this is for them."
